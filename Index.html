About

Jhenaidah Textile Engineering College is a government textile engineering college located in Jhenaidah, Bangladesh. There are currently a total of 10 government textile engineering colleges in the country. Jhenaidah Textile Engineering College is the only institution under the Department of Textiles that has been offering a direct B.Sc. in Engineering degree since its inception.

The college began its academic activities in the 2016â€“17 academic year with 120 students.

Located on 8 acres of land in Aruakandi beside the Jhenaidah-Magura highway, the campus is only 7 kilometers away from Jhenaidah town. After arriving at the Jhenaidah bus terminal from any part of Bangladesh, the campus becomes visible within 6 kilometers. The entire campus is surrounded by greenery. Behind the residential halls lies a beautiful natural open environment and a large pond. On the eastern side, there is a vast 3-acre playground, which is rare for any other campus. The security system of this campus is more advanced than that of most public educational institutions, with complete CCTV coverage. The college offers four departments under the Faculty of Textiles, affiliated with Bangladesh University of Textiles (BUTEX).
